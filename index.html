About

I am a biomedical informatics researcher in the Mayo Clinic ADVANCE Lab - Accelerating Discovery to Delivery via Technology Innovation for Clinical Excellence. My research focuses on (1) designing and validating natural language processing techniques for clinical information extraction, (2) developing informatics frameworks and processes to accelerate the secondary use of EHRs for clinical research and (3) discovering EHR heterogeneity and information quality through quantitative and qualitative methods.

I obtained my Ph.D. at the University of Minnesota, advised by Dr. Hongfang Liu. The title of my thesis is TRUST: Clinical Text Retrieval and Use towards Scientific Rigor and Transparent Process (PDF). Prior to that, I received my M.H.I. degree at the University of Michigan, School of Information and School of Public Health. I obtained my B.B.A. degree at the University of Iowa.
