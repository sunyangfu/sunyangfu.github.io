I am a biomedical informatics researcher in the Mayo ADVANCE lab My research focuses on (1) designing and validating natural language processing techniques for clinical information extraction, (2) developing informatics frameworks and processes to accelerate the secondary use of EHRs for clinical research and (3) discovering EHR heterogeneity through quantitative and qualitative methods.

I obtained my PhD at the University of Minnesota, advised by Dr. Hongfang Liu. Previously I was a master's student at the University of Michigan advised by Dr. Vinod Vydiswaran. I obtained my bachelor at the University of Iowa advised by Dr. Kang Zhao.
