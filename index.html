I am a researcher in the ADVANCE lab at Mayo Clinic, where I am supervised by Dr. Hongfang Liu. My research focuses on (1) designing and validating natural language processing techniques for clinical information extraction, (2) developing informatics frameworks to accelerate the secondary use of EHRs for clinical research and (3) discovering EHR heterogeneity through various computational methods.

Previously I was a master's student at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, where I was advised by Dr. Vinod Vydiswaran.
